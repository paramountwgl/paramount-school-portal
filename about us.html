Our Journey SINCE 2019

Located in the heart of Laxmipur, Warangal, Paramount English Medium School was founded with a singular vision: to bring world-class education to our community. What started as a vision by Mr. Shaik Ahmad has grown into a multi-disciplinary institution spanning polycet coaching, SI & PC training, and professional sports academies.

We believe that education should be accessible and technologically advanced. Our 2026 academic infrastructure is built on the pillars of safety, innovation, and result-oriented learning.
